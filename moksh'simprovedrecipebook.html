Brown Bread

  • Time: 5 hours
  • Serves: 4

Ingredients

1. 3 cups whole wheat

2. 1-1.25 cups water

3. 1 teaspoon instant yeast or 1/2 tbspn dry active yeast

4. 1 teaspoon regualar salt

5. 1 tablespoon regular sugar

6. 2 tablespoons of ghee or oil or butter

7. 2 tablespoons curd or 1.5 tablespoons vinegar.

8. Some milk for brushing the top of the bread.

Preparing the dough

1. Sieve the flour with salt or mix the flour with salt

2. Add 1 teaspoon instant yeast or lightly mix.

3. Then add sugar, oil ( or butter ) and curd.

4. First just mix everything lightly and then slowly adding water in intervalts knead to a smooth, soft dough.

5. Add more water in required. The dough should be slightly sticky

6. Brush some water all over the dough and keep in a deep wide bowl covered with a lid or kitchen towel for 30-35 minutes.

7. Remove and then lightly punch & knead the dough again.

8. Make a log of dough and seam the edges.

9. Place in greased loaf pan for 40-45 mins.

10. Cover and let it rise

11. Pre-heat the oven at 220 degrees celcius for at least 20 minutes.

12. Once the bread has risen in the loaf pan, keep in the oven and bake for 22-25 minutes or until the bread sounds hollow when tapped.

13. If the bread begins to brown from the top, then cover with butter paper or aluminum foil.

14. WHen whoel wheat bread is still hot, remove from the loaf pan and keep it on a wired tray to cool.

Preparing the dough with active dry yeast

1. Warm 1 or 1.25 cups of water.

2. Add 1 tablespoon sugar and dry active yeast.

3. Stir and let the yeast activate

4. This takes 10-12 minutes

5. Sieve noth the flour with the salt

6. Add the proofed yeast mixture, oil/butter/ghee and yogurt

7. Mix all the ingredients well

8. Then begin to knead the dough.

9. Add more water if required, the dough should be slightly sticky

10. Continue to knead, till you get a smooth dough which when stretched doesn't tear.

11. Rub or brush water all over the dough and keep in a convered bowl to leaver for 1.5-2 hours

12. After 1.5-2 hours, remove the dough and punch and deflate it lightly

13. Roll a singly log of the dough

14. Tuck the edges down on both sides of the bread loaf

15. Place the bread in a greased loaf pan (9*5") with edges facing down.

16. Cover the load pan and let the dough baven for 40 minutes to 1 hour.

17. Pre-heat oven to 220 degrees celcius for atleast 20 mins.

18. Once the bread has risen in the loaf pan, keep in the oven and bake bread for 25-22 minutes or until the bread sounds hollow when tapped.
